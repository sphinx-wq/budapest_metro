Subways of Budapest





How to Play

Build four metro lines on a 10×10 grid.

  • Draw a card (A/B/C/D/Joker)
  • Click an endpoint → click a matching station
  • Lines: up/down, left/right, or diagonal
  • Round ends after 8 cards
